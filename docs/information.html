Learning Network Security From the Pros

How do they manage billions of encoded lines every day?

In order to prevent everything from major viral infection to the tinniest of spam emails, knowing your own habits is key. You have to start prevention from the source. And that is yourself. We commonly don't read long disclaimers and see ourselves as pioneers of surfing the web. But there is a fatal truth. There will always be someone who is one step ahead, and someone who knows how you think and how to do it better. You're job is simply to make sure you never meet.

Server Protection

Find yourself swimming in a sea of code with no idea how to protect it all? Well hacking into a server or even a specific computer is harder than you think. Without a lot of decrypted information, it is vey difficult to access your PC remotely, let alone gain non-public information off of it. It may be able to track you, but rarely are they able to access you accounts.

Trust in the System

The World Wide Web is not without its flaws, but there is a degree of safety that has been taken to protect certain users experiences. The firewalls from Chrome and Firefox are highly likely to scan out any code that may be malicious to your computer systems. And most businesses have assigned data protection to prevent hacking accounts. There are even some services you can buy that create routine searches for your name of info on the deep web, a den for hackers.
